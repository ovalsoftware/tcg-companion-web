Everything in one app

Scan cards, see prices, track your collection. No typing set codes, no bouncing between tabs.

Price scanner

Point your camera at any English Pokémon card. TCG Companion identifies it and shows the current market price instantly.

Graded pricing

PSA 10, 9.5, 9, 8, 7 plus BGS 10, CGC 10 and SGC 10 — the tier that matches your condition.

Live eBay listings

Every card links straight to real eBay listings in your region — filter by sold, graded, or specific grade.

Collection tracking

Every card you scan rolls into a running collection with its current value. Export to CSV anytime.

Card variants

Every card is broken down into its variants — standard, reverse holo, holo, 1st edition, promo — so you log and price the exact print you own.

Multiple quantities

Got three copies of the same card? Log all three. Quantity is tracked per variant, so duplicates don't get lumped together in your totals.

Japanese cards

Japanese Pokémon cards are indexed too, with their own prices and separate set browser.
