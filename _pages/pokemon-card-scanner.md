faqs:
  - q: Does the scanner work in low light?
    a: Good lighting gives the most reliable results. The app outlines the card and prompts you to adjust if the angle or lighting is off.
  - q: Can it scan sleeved or top-loaded cards?
    a: Yes &mdash; most sleeves and toploaders don't interfere. Very reflective sleeves may need a small tilt to reduce glare.
  - q: Is there an Android version?
    a: TCG Companion is currently iOS-only. Meanwhile you can browse set lists and cards on this website.
